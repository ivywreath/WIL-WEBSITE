Garden Maintenance Course

This Garden Maintenance course provides essential knowledge and practical skills to handle emergency situations confidently. Learn how to perform CPR, treat wounds, and manage various medical emergencies effectively.

The purspose of this course is to provide basic knowledge of watering, pruning and planting in a domestic garden.

Fees: R750

Content:
-Water restrictions and the watering requirements of Indigenous and exotic plants.
-Prunning and propagation of plants.
-Planting techniques for different plant types
