Welcome to Empowering the Nation

Empowering the Nation is a small to mid-size enterprise founded by Precious Radebe. Our initiative is dedicated to providing skills training for domestic workers and gardeners.

Inspired by the struggles of her parents and elderly relatives, who never had the opportunity to upskill themselves or pursue formal education, Precious established this training school to support similarly affected members of her community.

The training we provide enables domestic workers and gardeners to become more marketable when seeking employment, earning higher pay due to their additional skills. Furthermore, they can become entrepreneurs by setting up their own small businesses utilizing these newly obtained skills.
